A Quiet Refuge For Body & Mind

Lumera is more than a spa — it is a sanctuary woven from sage gardens, candle-warm rooms, and rituals carried gently from teacher to therapist. Every treatment is shaped around your skin, your breath, your day.

  • ● Certified holistic therapists with 10+ years experience
  • ● Organic, locally-sourced botanical products
  • ● Private suites, infrared saunas & herbal steam rooms
  • ● Personalised wellness plans for every guest
